The **Fontainebleau Muslim Association (FMA)** is a Non-Profit Company (NPC) serving the Muslim community of Fontainebleau, Randburg, in Johannesburg, Gauteng, South Africa.

Located at **10 Silver Pine Avenue, Moret** (parallel to Rabie Street), the masjid is a spiritual home for the local community — a place of worship, learning, and togetherness.

## Our Mission

FMA exists to serve the spiritual, educational, and social needs of the Muslim community in the Fontainebleau area. We strive to provide a welcoming environment for all — from daily congregational Salaah to educational programmes and community support.

## Weekly Programmes

Our masjid offers regular programmes throughout the week, including Taleem sessions and Jumu'ah Bayaans. For the full schedule, visit the [Programmes](/programmes/) page.
